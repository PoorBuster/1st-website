Why we built FLT?

Though it may seem to be strange, our representative had some fate with Japan. After returning from studying abroad in Japan, he felt that Japanese people living in Hong Kong met various problems, and then he established the business with the desire to solve all their problems. That is why FLT is here today.

We provide comprehensive support for Japanese people living in Hong Kong (Japanese is available). We aim to support our customers with the goal of making them live comfortably in Hong Kong and becoming fond of Hong Kong. In addition to online customer support, we also provide services as a business advisor such as starting a business in Hong Kong.

We are here to support you all to make Hong Kong your second home!
